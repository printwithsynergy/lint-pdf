# Stripe product copy

Canonical descriptions for each LintPDF Stripe Product. Paste these into
the Product's **Description** field in the Stripe Dashboard (Production
and Sandbox). Descriptions propagate to Checkout, invoices, and the
Customer Portal, so it's worth keeping them readable for end users.

This file is the **single source of truth**. When you update a
description, update it here and then copy it into Stripe — otherwise
two people asking "what's this charge?" get two different answers.

## Subscription plans

Descriptions here are short because the plan comparison table on the
Billing page already covers the feature matrix.

### LintPDF Free
> Free tier for evaluation. 20 preflight runs / month, 10 MB max file
> size, community support. Upgrade any time — usage persists.

### LintPDF Viewer
> View, fill, and download annotated preflight reports without running
> your own jobs. Best for stakeholders reviewing submissions from
> designers who are on a higher tier.

### LintPDF Starter
> For small shops and freelancers. Unlimited rulesets, 500 preflight
> runs / month, 100 MB max file size, email support. Overage available
> if you spike past your monthly quota.

### LintPDF Growth
> For growing production teams. 2,500 preflight runs / month, 250 MB
> max file size, priority email support, webhook endpoints, API keys
> for automation. Overage available.

### LintPDF Scale
> For print operations with automated submit pipelines. 10,000
> preflight runs / month, 500 MB max file size, priority support,
> unlimited webhook endpoints, AI-assisted preflight included.

### LintPDF Enterprise
> Custom contracts with committed volume, custom domains per brand
> profile, SSO, audit log exports, SLAs. Contact sales for a quote.

## One-off packs (pay-as-you-go)

### AI Credits pack
> LintPDF AI Credits power optional AI-assisted preflight checks:
> branding validation, logo detection, image quality scoring, and
> layout QA. 1 credit = 1 AI check on a single page. Credits never
> expire and stack with your plan's monthly allotment.

### File Pack
> Extra preflight capacity on top of your monthly plan. Each pack adds
> N PDF submissions that roll over if unused. Use when you have a
> burst of reports (trade shows, end-of-quarter campaigns) without
> upgrading your base plan.
